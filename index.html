Common questions

A few things you
might be wondering.

How does Discover (the free plan) work?

You can add up to 5 reminders forever, no credit card required. You'll receive beautiful email reminders 7 days and 3 days before each occasion — enough notice to know it's coming.

What's the difference between Discover and Curate?

Discover (free) gives you up to 5 reminders with 7-day and 3-day email reminders — enough notice to know it's coming, but a genuinely tight window. Curate (£4.99/month) unlocks unlimited reminders with our full cadence (21, 14, 10, 7, 3-day and day-of), Just Because moments (recurring nudges to surprise loved ones), and access to our full partner brand collection.

Can I cancel Curate anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel your Curate subscription anytime from your account settings. No questions, no hard feelings. Your Discover reminders will continue working.

How do the gift recommendations work?

We've partnered with exceptional brands like Mappin & Webb, Jo Loves, and Vilebrequin. When a reminder is upcoming, we'll suggest gifts that suit the occasion — but you're always free to choose differently or just send a thoughtful message.

Will you share my data?

Never. Your reminders and the people you care about are private. We don't sell data, we don't share it with third parties, and we use the minimum information needed to send you beautiful reminders.

What if the platform isn't for me?

No problem at all. You can delete your account anytime from settings. Your data is removed completely, no questions asked.
