Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

At AGLAEA, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our service. We believe in transparency and want you to understand exactly what happens to your data.

1. Who We Are

AGLAEA ("we", "us", "our") operates the AGLAEA service available at aglaea.co.uk (the "Service"). We are the data controller responsible for your personal information.

Contact details:
Email: hello@aglaea.co.uk
Website: aglaea.co.uk

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you create an account and use AGLAEA, we collect:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and password
  • Optional contact details: Phone number (for SMS reminders if you opt in)
  • Reminder data: Names of people you add, relationships, important dates, and notes
  • Payment information: If you subscribe to Essential, we collect payment details via Stripe (we never see or store your full card details)

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage data: How you interact with our service
  • Device information: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system
  • Cookies: Essential cookies for authentication; we don't use tracking cookies for advertising

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the AGLAEA service
  • Send you reminder emails about important dates
  • Process your subscription payments (via Stripe)
  • Send service emails (welcome, password reset, upgrade confirmations)
  • Respond to your support enquiries
  • Improve our service and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, we process your data based on:

  • Contract: To provide the service you've signed up for
  • Consent: For optional features like SMS notifications or marketing emails
  • Legitimate interest: To improve our service and prevent fraud
  • Legal obligation: To comply with tax, accounting, and other legal requirements

5. Who We Share Data With

We share your information only with trusted third parties that help us provide our service:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and database storage
  • Stripe: Secure payment processing
  • Resend: Email delivery
  • Netlify: Website hosting and serverless functions

We never sell your data. We do not share your information with advertisers or marketing companies.

6. International Data Transfers

Some of our service providers may process data outside the UK/EU (such as in the US). Where this happens, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as:

  • EU Standard Contractual Clauses
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable
  • Certified frameworks like the EU-US Data Privacy Framework

7. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Your personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Some information may be retained longer for legal or accounting purposes
  • Anonymized usage data may be retained for analytics

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Object: Object to certain types of processing
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@aglaea.co.uk. We'll respond within 30 days.

9. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication, security, and functionality. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

10. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure password hashing
  • Regular security reviews
  • Access controls for our team

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we strive to protect your data using industry-standard practices.

11. Children's Privacy

AGLAEA is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

12. Marketing Communications

We may send you occasional marketing emails about AGLAEA. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any marketing email or by contacting us.

Service emails (reminders, account notifications) are not marketing and you cannot opt out while your account is active.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or a notice on our website. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

14. Complaints

If you have concerns about how we handle your data, please contact us first at hello@aglaea.co.uk. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO):
